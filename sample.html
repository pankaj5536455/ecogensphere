soil_and_climate

Red loamy soil with good drainage, pH range 6.5 to 8, dry climate with July to December planting season

planting_material

Plantable size grafts propagated through approach, soft wood or epicotyl grafting

field_preparation

Dig pits of 1 m x 1 m x 1 m, fill with topsoil mixed with 10 kg of FYM

irrigation

Regular watering till establishment, adopt drip system of irrigation for cultivation under irrigated conditions

manures_and_fertilizers

Manures and fertilizers applied during September – October, 45 – 90 cm away from the trunk upto the peripheral leaf tip and incorporated

planting

Plant grafts at the center of the pits with ball of earth intact, stake and water immediately after planting

spacing

Spacing options include 7x7m or 10x10m under conventional system, 5 m x 5 m for High Density Planting, 3 x 2 m for Ultra High Density Planting, and 5 m x 5 m within double rows for Double hedge row system

canopy_management

Remove rootstock sprouts and low lying branches nearer to ground, prune judiciously during August – September once in three years

top_working

Use scions of choice varieties for top working, behead trees during July- August and graft during September- October

growth_regulators

Spray NAA @ 20 ppm at flowering, spray Urea or Potassium nitrate during February to induce flowering, spray KNO3 at mustard size to increase fruit size, spray sulphate of potash at pea stage and 15 days after to improve yield and quality, use Paclobutrazol during off year for maximum yield

off_season_crop_induction

To induce off-season flowering, head back terminal growth after emergence of new growth during December - January, soil application of Paclobutrazol during March - April is recommended
